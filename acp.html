Chole bhature

ingredient

The main ingredients for Chole Bhature are: chickpeas (for the chole), all-purpose flour (maida) for the bhature, onions, garlic, ginger, cumin seeds, coriander powder, turmeric powder, chili powder, garam masala, oil, salt, and curd (for kneading the bhature dough).
